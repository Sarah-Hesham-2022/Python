1) BOW:
   
   Bag of words, the one used in my code, based on creating an array of all unique words in data set
   and counting number of occurrence of each word in the text, based on its index saved.

2) Term Frequency :

   is defined as the ratio of number of times a word appears in a document to total 
   number of words in that document.

3) Inverse Document Frequency :

  is defined as log of ratio of number of documents in the corpus to number of documents 
  in which the word has occurred.
